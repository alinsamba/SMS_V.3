# SMS-V.3 Website

This repository contains the public-facing website for SMS-V.3, a school management platform positioned around administration, academic records, communication, and leadership visibility.

The site is a Vite + React presentation layer. It is not the full school management application codebase. Its job is to present the product clearly, explain the platform scope, and support demos, pricing conversations, and rollout planning.

## Problems And Benefits Now Explained

The website now makes a clearer case for why a school might need SMS-V.3, not just what modules exist.

The messaging now highlights problems such as:

- Scattered records across paper, spreadsheets, and chat tools
- Slow operational follow-up caused by disconnected information
- Limited visibility for administrators and school leaders

The site also explains benefits such as:

- Centralized school records and workflows
- Better staff coordination
- Clearer visibility into operations and academic progress
- A more professional operating model for schools modernizing their processes
